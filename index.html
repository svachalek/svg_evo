Vector Painting Evolution

This page starts by creating a set of paintings that each contain one oval or triangle. These paintings are mutated by adding, removing, or altering components, and also cross-bred with other paintings. The results are compared to the image labeled "Target", paying particular attention to areas shown in the image labeled "Weights". The dark areas in the "Differences" image show where the target image is most different from the best solution. The best image and a selection of the others are retained, the rest are thrown out, and the process repeats.
